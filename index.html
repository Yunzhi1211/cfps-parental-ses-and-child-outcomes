CFPS Parental SES Project

How Do Parental Socioeconomic Resources Shape Children's Occupational Outcomes?

Using CFPS 2010–2022, this project studies how parental SES affects children's occupational outcomes across quantiles, regions, and time, with a focus on human capital and social capital mechanisms.
